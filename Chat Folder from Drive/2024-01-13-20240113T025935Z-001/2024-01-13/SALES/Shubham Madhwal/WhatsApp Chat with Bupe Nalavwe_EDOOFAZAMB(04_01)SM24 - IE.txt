*What is it About?*

Aerospace Engineering deals with aircraft that stay within the atmosphere of earth and also with those that operate outside the earth’s atmosphere. It is an applied discipline of engineering dealing with making, designing, manufacturing, functioning, and maintenance of vehicles such as aircraft, spacecraft, and missiles.

BTech Aerospace Engineering involves the study and practice of physical science and mathematics to perform research. The core areas of specialization for a team of aerospace engineers include aerodynamics, thermodynamics, control system, propulsion, celestial mechanics, and electronics.
